022 * the given component. In addition it is capable of rendering the 'id'-attribute as 'id' and

024 * {@link UIComponent#getId()} is set, the 'styleClass' and 'style' attributes and reacts to the

124    private void writeIdAndName(final FacesContext context, final UIComponent component) throws IOException {
